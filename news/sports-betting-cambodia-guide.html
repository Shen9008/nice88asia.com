Asian Handicap Explained

Asian handicap betting gives one team a virtual head start to create more balanced odds. For example, -0.5 means the team must win by at least one goal for your bet to win. +0.5 means the team can draw or win. The draw is eliminated as an outcome, making it a two-way market. Asian handicap is available for football, basketball, and other sports. It is favoured by experienced bettors for its value and reduced variance compared to 1X2.
