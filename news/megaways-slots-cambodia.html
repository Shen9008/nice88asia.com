How Megaways Slots Work

Megaways replaces fixed paylines with a dynamic system. Each reel can display 2 to 7 symbols (the number changes every spin). The number of ways to win is calculated by multiplying the symbols on each reel. For example, 3x4x5x4x3x2 = 2,880 ways. Maximum ways (7x7x7x7x7x7) = 117,649. Winning combinations are formed by matching symbols on adjacent reels from left to right. Cascading or tumble mechanics often apply—winning symbols disappear and new ones fall, creating chain reactions. This creates exciting, volatile gameplay.

Are Megaways slots high or low volatility?

Most Megaways slots are high volatility. Wins can be large but less frequent. The cascade feature can create chains of wins, but dry spells are also common. Set a budget suitable for volatile play.

What is the cascade or tumble feature?

When you form a winning combination, those symbols disappear and new ones fall from above. This can create additional wins from a single spin. Cascades continue until no new wins form. Multipliers often increase with each cascade in bonus rounds.
